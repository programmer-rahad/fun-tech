About Funtech studio

Funtech studio is a development studio specialized in web3. We are currently developing the first commercial metaverse and several innovative NFT collections. Our mission is to revolutionize the user experience during the online shopping process.

Join us

Metaverse

Funtech's mission is to create and optimize the best shopping experience for consumers and brands in the metaverse.

Service 1

Creation of nft in collaboration with brands and resale via the marketplace.

Service 2

3D store creation for brands and retailers.

Service 3

Creation of the brand's products in 3D, augmented reality and NFT format.

Service 4

Creation of 3D events in the platform (concert, NFT museum, promotion of a brand or product).

Service 5

Creating an immersive, fun and social user experience.

Service 6

Creation of a news feed where consumers can react to different brands, products, events of the metaverse.
